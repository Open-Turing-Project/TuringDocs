Operator Precedence

Highest precedence operators first.
  1. **, ^, #
  2. prefix + and -
  3. * , / , div , mod , rem , shl , shr
  4. + , -, xor
  5. < , > , = , <= , >= , not= , in , not in
  6. not
  7. and
  8. or
  9. =>
